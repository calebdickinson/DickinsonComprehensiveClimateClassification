Here's a quick search for climates. Pink buttons represent climates that currently exist, and green buttons represent climate groups that currently exist. Red buttons represent climates that do not exist now but will exist in 2100. Black buttons represent climates that do not exist and will not exist in 2100, but are part of the Dickinson climate classification. It is possible that the climates represented by the black buttons existed in the earth's distant past or may exist in its future and/or they may exist on faraway planets. The climate of the planet Mercury, for example, would fall under the YH category (superarctic hypercaneal summer).
